Before you begin...

ChordAce works best in Google Chrome and Opera. If you can't get your midi keyboard to work, try using one of those browsers.

If you don't have a midi-keyboard connected you can use your computers keyboard, but a midi keyboard is recommended.

This app is made by Morten Vestergaard and Webfar using Vanilla JavaScript with Tonal.js, Howler.js and VexFlow.

Have fun learning and teaching!

Play your connected MIDI device or use your computer keyboard.
